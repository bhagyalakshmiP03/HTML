Steps to prepare tea

  1. Boil water in a kettle
  2. Add tea leaves to the boiling water
  3. Let it steep for 3-5 minutes
  4. Add sugar and milk to taste
  5. Strain the tea into a cup
  6. Enjoy your cup of tea
